Juggernaut – Bladeform Legacy Arcana

This is Juggernaut’s special Arcana from Dota 2.

The following image is the full Bladeform Legacy:

Blade Fury Healing Ward Blade Dance Omnislash

Blade Fury

Juggernaut spins rapidly, dealing magic damage and becoming immune to spells.

Healing Ward

Summons a healing ward that restores health to nearby allies over time.

Blade Dance

Passive ability that grants critical strike chance on attacks.

Omnislash

Juggernaut leaps between enemies, slashing them at lightning speed.
